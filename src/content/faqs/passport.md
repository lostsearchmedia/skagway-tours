---
question: Do I really need a passport?
category: Passports & Border
order: 10
---

Yes. Every Skagway Tours route crosses into Canada. US and Canadian citizens
need a valid passport, NEXUS card, or approved alternative. International
guests generally need a passport and any required visa. No passport, no tour,
no kidding — see our [passport requirements](/passport-requirements/) page for
full details.
