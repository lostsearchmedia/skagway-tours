Klondike Prospector Protection was instated as a means for customers to protect their purchase on Skagway Tours. With Prospector coverage added to your reservation, you get extra protection for changes in plans and can receive a full refund — with no change fees — up to **6 hours before tour time**.

Prospector Protection is especially handy when travelers are forced to cancel *after* the standard 14-day cancellation cut-off. Without it, refunds cannot be issued after the cut-off.

Prospector Protection is **only** for tours operated by Skagway Tours directly. It may not apply to affiliates or tours in other parts of the state.

### Fees & conditions

The Prospector Protection fee is based on the cost of each tour and varies by tour type. **The fee itself is non-refundable.** The option to add Prospector Protection is visible in the lower section of the booking form for every eligible tour.

### Terms

- Prospector Protection can only be added **at the time of original booking** — it cannot be applied after purchase.
- It is only valid on bookings made directly with Skagway Tours. Bookings made through third parties do not qualify.
- It is not valid for bookings made inside the Prospector Protection cut-off window for that tour's start date or departure time.

### How to claim a refund with Prospector Protection

- Contact Skagway Tours by phone or email at least the Prospector Protection term **prior** to the day-tour departure time (see your confirmation for specifics).
- Provide the booking number from your confirmation email to the reservation agent. You'll receive a full refund, minus the non-refundable Prospector Protection fee.
- Prospector Protection does **not** cover no-shows or late arrivals. If you contact us inside the protection window, coverage will not apply.
