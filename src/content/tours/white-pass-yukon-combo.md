Our flagship day out combines the legendary White Pass railway with a Yukon bus
tour that most Skagway day-trippers never get to see. You'll ride the train up
through the switchbacks and tunnels of the Gold Rush route, cross into British
Columbia at the summit, and continue by bus to Emerald Lake and the Carcross
Desert.

## What to bring

- Valid passport (required — we cross the Canadian border).
- Layers — it's often 20°F cooler at the summit than at the dock.
- Camera with extra battery.

## What's included

- All transport (train + bus).
- Driver-guide narration the whole way.
- Lunch stop in Carcross.
- Border fees.
