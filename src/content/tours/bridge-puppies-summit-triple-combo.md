This exclusive Skagway Tours triple combo feels like one shared story with your guide, told in three unforgettable chapters. Your day starts in town as your driver-guide gets to know your group and eases you up the Klondike Highway with a mix of Gold Rush tales, quiet stretches, and scenic stops. High in the mountains, wide-open summit views set the tone before you continue deeper into Canada.

At a remote sled dog camp, the mood shifts to pure "awww" as you meet Alaskan Husky puppies and hear how these future athletes are raised and trained. Then comes the calm thrill of the Yukon Suspension Bridge, where you stand safely above swift rapids and read stories about the Mounties who once kept order on this wild route. It's our highest-rated tour, great for families and groups, with something for everyone.

## What's included

- Land tour via 13-passenger bus
- Entry to the Tagish Lake Sled Dog Camp
- Entry to the Yukon Suspension Bridge
- Multiple photo stops along Klondike Highway, including "Welcome to Alaska" sign, White Pass Summit, and "Welcome to Yukon" sign
- Pickup & drop-off at cruise docks
- Our All Aboard guarantee — we run on ship time and have never missed a departure

## What's not included

Lunch isn't included, but you're welcome to bring food and drinks to enjoy on the bus — or grab something from the café at the bridge.

## Itinerary

**Departure**

- Meet outside your ship or at Centennial Park

**En route**

- Driver-guide commentary
- Multiple photo stops along the Klondike Highway
- "Welcome to Alaska" sign
- White Pass Summit
- Cross into Canada

**Destination 1 — Sled Dog Camp**

- 30 minutes at Tutshi Lake Sled Dog Camp
- Play with puppies and learn what it takes to raise a race winner

**Destination 2 — Yukon Suspension Bridge**

- 45 minutes at the bridge
- Walk across, explore, enjoy the café
- Bison chili optional but highly recommended

**Continuing**

- "Welcome to the Yukon" sign
- Tagish Lake views
- Tormented Valley

**Return**

You choose: get dropped off at your ship or in town. Just let the driver know.

## What to bring

- Layered clothing — rainforest meets alpine tundra
- Waterproof jacket
- Comfortable, closed-toe shoes
- Camera
- $1 bills or quarters for musher-approved puppy treats
- Cash or card for optional snacks and souvenirs

> **No passport, no tour, no kidding.** Every Skagway Tours route crosses into Canada. Arriving without your passport means riding straight back to the ship, and missed tours aren't refundable.
